TYPES OF COMPUTER AND THEIR ADVANTAGES

TOWER MODEL -refers to a computer in which the power supply, motherboard,and mass storage devices are stacked on top of each other

Advantage

  • fewer space
  • easy installation of additional storage devices

DESKTOP MODEL - is designed to fit on top of a desk they are broad and low. they are limited to three internal mass storage devices

Advantage

  • small
  • slim

LAPTOP COMPUTER - is a lightweight personal computer that can sit on your lap

Advantage

  • weight less than 6 pounds
  • fits easily in a brief case
  • comes with battery packs
  • attached display screen

NETBOOK COMPUTER - is a slightly lighter and smaller type of a laptop computer

Advantage

  • smaller keyboard
  • smaller screen
  • can run without plugging in

HAND HELD COMPUTER - is a small computer enough to be held in one hand. it has function of a calendar and address book.

Advantage

  • convenient to carry
  • a personal information manager
